Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Lemowaves AI is built by Sam and designed with your privacy in mind.

  • No accounts required. You get a unique local ID automatically — no email, no sign-up.
  • Chat history is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device.
  • Messages sent to the AI pass through Pollinations.ai API to generate responses. We do not log or store these on our servers.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no cookies, no third-party trackers.
  • Images you upload are processed in your browser and sent to the AI API for description. They are not stored.

Questions? Contact Sam, the creator of Lemowaves AI.
